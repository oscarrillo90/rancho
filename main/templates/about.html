About

Rancho Grande Bar & Grill was originated in 1991 by the Carrillo Brothers who came from Guadalajara, the land where most of the tequela is cultivated and the mariachi music lives in your blood. With original recipes like the Chile Relleno and new dishes like the Chicken Diablo... Rancho Grande became a favorite for thousands fo enjoy!
